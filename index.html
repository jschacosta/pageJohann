    Laucha app is a project developed as a Vue 2 js spa. The focus is to create a tournament to play with friends to guess sports results. Each match can have its own rules and different scores, and according to the predictions, players are ranked upon entering the game. You can visit the demo as a player or as an administrator.
    SOS Travel is a fully operational application that serves as a bridge between workers, hotels/hostels, and travelers. Its purpose is to enable travelers to effortlessly book and schedule services in just three simple steps, while allowing workers the flexibility to accept or decline job offers as they see fit. The platform includes a secure payment gateway, real-time chat functionality powered by socket.io, and tailored interfaces for clients, workers, and business users (hostels/hotels).
    Unabase Grid is an application where I currently lead the team in developing a new version of the company's existing software. This app empowers businesses to generate quotes using a grid crafted entirely with HTML and CSS, eliminating the need for external libraries. Furthermore, it facilitates the creation of specialized formulas, supports multiple currencies, applies diverse tax rates, and offers the flexibility to print quotes in various formats tailored to the needs of the business.
    Homepage for a ceramic artist. The central aim was to craft a visually striking platform that highlights the intricate beauty and skillful craftsmanship of the artist's creations.
